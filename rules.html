Rules & Regulations

Booth Construction

1. Maximum height for bare space booths is 2.5m.
2. All contractors must wear safety vests during setup.
3. No drilling into the hall floor is permitted.

Electrical Safety

1. Do not use multi-plug extensions (heavy usage risks tripping).
2. Only certified electricians allowed for wiring works.
3. All electrical items must be SIRIM approved.
